Research.

Developing the Harmfulness Score (H Score): A Novel Framework for Evaluating Packaged Food Content

S. Akshay · Amruthaluri Yuvadeep · M. K. Shreevatsa · B. M. Laveen · S. Hemanth Bharadwaj

A novel framework combining OCR-based text extraction, nutritional analysis and NOVA food classification to compute a standardized harmfulness index from packaged food labels. Designed to work reliably on low-quality, distorted images, validated against Nutri-Score, and aimed at empowering healthier dietary decisions.
